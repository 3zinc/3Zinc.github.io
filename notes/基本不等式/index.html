    基本不等式¶

    \(a+b\geq2\sqrt{ab}\)

    推导¶

    \(\because (a-b)^2 \geq 0\)

    \(\therefore a^2-2ab+b^2 \geq 0\)

    \(\therefore a^2+2ab+b^2 \geq 4ab\)

    \(\therefore (a+b)^2 \geq 4ab\)

    \(\therefore a+b \geq 2\sqrt{ab}\)

    延伸¶

    \(a^2+b^2 \geq \dfrac{1}{2}(a+b)^2\)
